Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about reservations, room facilities, dining, and hotel services to help you plan a smooth and comfortable stay with us.

01

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is available from 2:00 PM, and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged upon request, subject to availability.

02

Does the hotel provide free Wi-Fi and parking?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, and free on-site parking is provided for all guests.

03

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, free breakfast is included with your stay and served at our open-air restaurant, offering a selection of freshly prepared dishes.

04

Does the hotel have a swimming pool?

Yes, Eco Black and White Hotel features a well-maintained outdoor swimming pool with sun loungers, perfect for relaxation in a peaceful environment.

05

How far is the hotel from major attractions?

Eco Black and White Hotel is located in Mihintale, just 11 km from Ruwanweli Maha Seya and 12 km from Anuradhapura Nature Park, offering easy access to the region's most iconic heritage sites.
